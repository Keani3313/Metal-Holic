Statement — 01

A festival for the loud part of growing up.

MetalHolic is a Nu Metal and alternative festival built around the music that shaped a generation in the early 2000s — the records people taped, traded and burned to CD-R, the bands that didn’t fit on glossy magazine covers but shaped the emotional vocabulary of a whole scene.

Three days, three states: atmosphere, chaos, legacy. We treat the festival as an editorial archive — not a brand activation. Programming is curated like a magazine issue, not a streaming playlist.

Statement — 02

A magazine, not a brand activation

The festival is documented as a music editorial archive. Each edition becomes a printed issue — features, photo essays, transcripts — filed permanently in the MetalHolic Library.

Statement — 03

Identity, emotion, individuality

MetalHolic is built around expression and difference. The crowd is the festival — not the target audience. Capacity is limited so the room can be the room and not a livestream backdrop.

Statement — 04

One continuous emotional arc

Performances are programmed without pauses between stages. Atmosphere bleeds into chaos, chaos folds into legacy. Silence is composed, never accidental.
