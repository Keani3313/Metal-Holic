Conditions

Read before you enter the queue.

Tickets are nominal. The name on the ticket must match a valid government-issued ID at the gate. One ticket per person. Re-sale is permitted only through the official MetalHolic resale platform; tickets sold through third parties may be voided without refund.

Refunds are issued only in the event of full-festival cancellation. Schedule modifications, single-artist cancellations, weather conditions or partial line-up changes do not constitute grounds for refund. Children under 14 enter free with an accompanying adult holding a General Pass.

For accessible-access requests, group bookings or formal industry accreditation, contact tickets@metalholic.fest.
