1 — What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. It allows the site to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time. Similar technologies (local storage, pixels, fingerprints) are treated as cookies in this policy.

2 — What kinds we use

2.1 Strictly necessary

Required for the website to function: session continuity, ticket-cart state, and security tokens. These cannot be disabled and do not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive.

2.2 Functional

Optional cookies that remember your preferences (language, dark/light, accepted-cookies state). Set only with your consent.

2.3 Analytical

Aggregated, non-identifying statistics on which pages are visited, how long, and from which referrer. Set only with your consent. We use a privacy-respecting, EU-hosted analytics provider; we do not use Google Analytics on this site.

2.4 Forum cookies

Cookies set by the forum component to keep you logged in, remember your reading position and your "mark as read" state. Set on the Forum page only and only after registration.

4 — Your consent

On your first visit, a banner asks you to accept, refuse or configure non-essential cookies. Strictly necessary cookies are set in any case, as permitted by Article 5(3) of Directive 2002/58/EC.

You may change your consent at any time by clicking the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer or by clearing the mh_consent cookie in your browser.

5 — Managing & deleting cookies

Most browsers let you view, manage, refuse and delete cookies. Refusing strictly-necessary cookies will degrade the website (you may not be able to purchase tickets, post on the forum or stay logged in).

  • Firefox — Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari — Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies

6 — Third-party services

Embedded video, payment processing and ticket fulfilment may set their own cookies if you choose to use those services. The relevant providers are listed on the Privacy Policy page, together with their data-protection contacts.

7 — Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the website, the festival, or applicable law. Material changes will be communicated via a notice on the home page for at least fifteen (15) days. The most recent version is always available at this URL.
