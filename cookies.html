COOKIES POLICY

This Cookies Policy explains how MetalHolic Festival uses cookies and similar technologies on its website to enhance user experience and analyze interaction patterns within the platform.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand navigation behavior and improve functionality.

HOW WE USE THEM

We use cookies to maintain session stability, analyze traffic trends, and personalize the browsing experience within the festival website structure.

THIRD PARTY COOKIES

Some cookies may be placed by external services such as embedded media players or analytics tools. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

CONTROL

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain functionalities of the site.
