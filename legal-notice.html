6 — Tickets & entry conditions

Tickets purchased through this website are governed by the General Terms of Sale, available on the Tickets page and accepted by the buyer at the moment of purchase. Tickets are nominal and may be subject to identity verification at the gate.

Entry to the festival venue is conditional on compliance with the venue rules, applicable safety regulations and the instructions of the festival staff. The Organiser reserves the right to refuse entry without refund in the event of breach of these rules.

7 — Claims & dispute resolution

Any complaint relating to the website, ticketing or the festival itself shall be addressed in writing to claims@metalholic.fest. The Organiser will acknowledge receipt within ten (10) working days and propose a resolution within thirty (30) working days.

For consumers resident in the European Union, the European Commission online dispute-resolution platform is accessible at ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.

8 — Applicable law

This website and all relations between the Organiser and its users or attendees are governed by the law of the Russian Federation, without prejudice to mandatory consumer-protection provisions applicable in the user's country of habitual residence.

Disputes that cannot be resolved amicably will be submitted to the competent courts at the registered office of the Organiser, subject to mandatory rules to the contrary.
