Press
Archive

MetalHolic is presented as a hybrid between festival and cultural institution. Coverage is encouraged to focus on architecture, sound curation and spatial narrative — not on entertainment framing.

The Red Square edition is a fictional reconstruction of collective memory through live performance. The press archive collects the festival's documentation: programmes, manifestos, photographs, transcripts and obituaries. Each edition is filed and kept permanent.

Working press receive accreditation through the Crew Pass program. Accreditation includes access to the Press & Archive room beneath the Main stage — a working library with film scans, oral histories and a small editing bay.

Embargoes apply to all artist interviews and recorded performances. The festival reserves the right to revoke accreditation in cases of unauthorised distribution of recorded material.
