Lineupthree days, three states

Three days, three states. 15 — 17 August, 14:00 — 23:00 daily. The lineup is programmed as one continuous emotional arc — from quiet density into nu-metal core, and finally into the records that shaped the whole vocabulary of alternative metal.

01

AtmosphereGrunge / Post-Grunge / Alternative Rock

Saturday 15.08

The opening day is a slow descent into sound. One headline name, then an emotional, more nicheous trajectory. Internal noise becomes external architecture; the audience does not enter a concert, it enters a climate. Volume is secondary — texture, density and quiet tension are the point.

ChaosNu Metal / Alternative Metal / Groove Metal

Sunday 16.08

Day two is the loud one. Two big names anchor the night and the rest is the real underground of nu-metal — the bands that lived the scene from inside, far from the algorithm. The architecture begins to fracture under the weight of its own resonance.

Main
