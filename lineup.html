Lineupthree days, three states

Atmosphere on Thursday. Chaos on Friday. Legacy on Saturday. The lineup is programmed as one continuous emotional arc — from quiet density into nu-metal core, and finally into the records that shaped the whole vocabulary of alternative metal. Times are indicative and subject to last-minute curatorial drift.

01

AtmosphereEmotional / atmospheric

Thursday 14.08
Main · Atmosphere · Night Session

The opening day is a slow descent into sound. One headline name, then an emotional, more nicheous trajectory. Internal noise becomes external architecture; the audience does not enter a concert, it enters a climate. Volume is secondary — texture, density and quiet tension are the point.
