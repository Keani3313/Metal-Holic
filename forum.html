METALHOLIC FORUM

The MetalHolic Forum is a digital extension of the festival space where visitors exchange ideas, experiences, and interpretations of the event. It functions as a living archive of audience perception.

RULES

Respectful communication is required. Harassment, hate speech, or disruptive behavior is not tolerated. This space is designed for discussion, not conflict.
