Welcome back. This is the only official live thread for the 2026 edition. Please post short observations from the floor — no setlists, no recordings, no spoilers from artists who haven't played yet.

The granite holds heat. The square is louder than you remember. Be kind to first-timers and to security; they are tired before you arrive.

★ Sticky rules: no personal attacks · no resale links · no recorded material · respect the silence between sets — it is composed, not accidental.
