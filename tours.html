Touring
Editions

MetalHolic operates a year-round circuit of satellite events, each interpreting the festival's archive in a different city. The touring editions are not greatest-hits packages — they are situational reconstructions.

Confirmed cities · 2026

Six chapters, one continuous archive.

★ 01 · BARCELONA

Mediterranean Edition

12 — 14.06.2026 · Parc del Fòrum

An open-air dialogue with Primavera Sound's spatial logic. Two-stage layout, post-metal and screamo focus.

★ 02 · BERLIN

Experimental Stage

04 — 06.07.2026 · Kraftwerk Berlin

Industrial archive with extended drone and noise residencies. Hosted inside a decommissioned power plant.

★ 03 · TOKYO

Night Sessions

26 — 28.09.2026 · Liquidroom + Wall&Wall

Late-night curated program of Japanese post-metal, drone and dark ambient. No daylight performances.

★ 04 · MEXICO CITY

Brutalist Tour

17 — 19.10.2026 · Foro Indie Rocks!

Hardcore-leaning weekend with regional Latin American programming and a permanent zine fair.

★ 05 · WARSAW

Eastern Block

14 — 16.11.2026 · Hala Koło

A focused dialogue between post-Soviet and Polish underground scenes. Three days, two stages, no headliners.

★ 06 · BUENOS AIRES

South Cone Closing Rite

12 — 14.02.2027 · Niceto Club

Final chapter of the cycle — a compressed three-day reflection on the year's archive, inviting the entire ensemble back.
