MI METALHOLIC

MetalHolic is not defined as a traditional festival, but as a cultural reconstruction of sound environments. It exists between performance and archive, where music becomes documentation of emotional and spatial states.

Each edition is built around a fictional geopolitical environment — Red Square — which acts as a symbolic space for tension, memory, and collective perception.

The festival does not aim to entertain in a conventional sense, but to preserve sound as cultural evidence.
