PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explains how MetalHolic Festival ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, purchase tickets, or participate in festival activities.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in various ways:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Ticket Purchases: Billing address, payment information, ticket preferences
  • Contact Forms: Name, email, message content, subject matter
  • Newsletter Signup: Email address for festival updates and promotions
  • Survey Responses: Feedback and preferences about the festival

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, scroll behavior
  • Location Data: IP address (for security and analytics purposes)
  • Cookies: Session cookies and tracking cookies (see our Cookies Policy)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling ticket orders
  • Sending confirmation emails and transaction receipts
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improving our website and festival experience
  • Sending promotional emails and festival updates (with consent)
  • Analyzing usage patterns to optimize our services
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring website security
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process ticket transactions
  • Email Service Providers: To send newsletters and festival updates
  • Hosting Providers: For website infrastructure and security
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

4. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, typically:

  • Ticket purchase data: 2 years after event completion
  • Account information: Until you request deletion
  • Marketing emails: Until you unsubscribe
  • Usage analytics: Anonymized after 12 months

5. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data in a readable format.

5.2 Correction

You can request corrections to inaccurate information.

5.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.

5.4 Opt-Out

You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Secure password storage using encryption algorithms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

For detailed information about cookies and how we use tracking technologies, please see our Cookies Policy.

8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws than your home country. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the revised policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@metalholic.com
  • Contact Form: Send a message
