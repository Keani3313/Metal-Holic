FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about MetalHolic Festival

Tickets & Passes

When do tickets go on sale?

+

Early bird tickets are now available with a 40% discount. General admission tickets will be released on a rolling basis. Check our News section for the latest announcements regarding ticket release dates and pricing tiers.

What are the different ticket types?

+

Day Pass: Single-day access to all performances

3-Day Pass: Full festival access with all three days

VIP Pass: Premium access with exclusive areas and merchandise

Student Discount: Available with valid ID (10% off)

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

+

Tickets are non-transferable and are tied to the purchaser's name. The ticket holder must present valid ID matching the name on the ticket at entry. For exceptional circumstances, contact our support team.

What is your refund policy?

+

Full refunds are available until 30 days before the festival. After that date, tickets may only be exchanged for a different date (if applicable). No refunds will be issued for cancellations due to weather or unforeseen circumstances beyond our control.

Accommodation & Travel

Is accommodation included with the ticket?

+

No, accommodation is not included with festival tickets. However, we provide recommendations for hotels, hostels, and camping options near Red Square. Visit our Travel section for partnered accommodations with special festival rates.

How do I get to the festival venue?

+

By Air: Fly into Moscow's main airports (Sheremetyevo, Domodedovo, or Vnukovo), then take metro or taxi to Red Square.

By Train: Moscow has excellent rail connections. Several trains daily arrive at central stations.

Public Transport: The Moscow Metro is the easiest way to reach the venue.

Do you offer group discounts?

+

Yes! Groups of 10 or more receive a 15% discount. Contact our group sales team at groups@metalholic.com with your group details for a customized quote.

Festival Experience

What should I bring to the festival?

+

Essentials: Valid ID, ticket confirmation, comfortable clothing, sturdy shoes, and weather protection.

Recommended: Sunscreen, water bottle, power bank, comfortable backpack, and cash/cards.

Not Allowed: Professional cameras (single-lens reflex), pets (except service animals), and illegal substances.

What is the age requirement?

+

MetalHolic Festival is open to attendees 16 years and older. Those under 18 must be accompanied by an adult. Valid ID is required at entry.

Can I bring outside food and beverages?

+

Outside beverages are not permitted, but empty water bottles can be filled at provided stations. Non-perishable food items in unopened packaging are allowed. Festival food vendors offer a variety of options at all price points.

Is there an accessibility program for disabled visitors?

+

Yes, MetalHolic Festival is committed to accessibility. We offer wheelchair-accessible areas, accessible restrooms, and assistive listening devices. Contact accessibility@metalholic.com at least 30 days before the festival to request accommodations.

Merchandise & Shop

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over €50)

Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (€12 flat rate)

International: 7-14 business days (varies by location)

What is your return policy?

+

Unworn merchandise can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for orders over €50. Contact shop@metalholic.com to initiate a return.

Are items available at the festival venue?

+

Yes! Our official merchandise booth will be open during festival hours with limited stock of popular items. Sizes and availability vary. We recommend pre-ordering online if you want a specific item.

Safety & Policies

What are the weather contingency plans?

+

The festival will proceed rain or shine. Stages are equipped with protective covers. In case of extreme weather, performances may be rescheduled. Monitor our News section and social media for real-time updates.

What is your COVID-19 policy?

+

We follow all local health regulations and recommendations. Health protocols may include hand sanitization stations, distancing guidelines, and mask recommendations. Check our website closer to the festival for any updated requirements.

How do I report a safety concern?

+

Our security team is visible throughout the venue. Report any concerns to the nearest staff member or visit the Information Booth. You can also email safety@metalholic.com after the festival to report incidents.
